Claude Mythos Preview Finds Zero-Days in Every Major OS — Apple, Microsoft, Amazon Join Project Glasswing Consortium

Anthropic unveiled Claude Mythos Preview, a model so capable at cybersecurity that the company chose not to release it publicly. The model remains restricted, but the goal is to eventually enable safe deployment of Mythos-class capabilities at scale.
