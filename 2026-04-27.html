At Google Cloud Next 2026, Google unveiled official Model Context Protocol servers for Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, and Google Chat — all now in developer preview. This means any third-party AI agent, from Claude to custom enterprise bots, can securely read emails, search files, schedule meetings, and send chat messages through a standardized protocol.

The move is a radical shift in Google's API strategy. Alongside this, Google announced "Workspace Intelligence" — a unified semantic understanding layer that powers agentic work across Docs, Slides, Gmail, and Calendar.
